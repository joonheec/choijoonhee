    • We are Pi Delta Psi of NYU.

    Pi Delta Psi is the fastest growing Asian American Fraternity of its kind. Each Chapter is fully recognized by their respective institutions, reflecting our commitment to cooperate with administrators and the overall campus community.

    Pi Delta Psi and its members seek to bring about Asian-American unity by breaking down cultural barriers amongst Asian communities by upholding and/or instilling the fraternities pillars: academic achievement, cultural awareness, righteousness, friendship, and loyalty.

    Cultural Awareness

    Cultural Awareness is the foundation of communication and it involves the ability of standing back from ourselves and becoming aware of our cultural values, beliefs and perceptions.

    Academic Achievement

    Academic achievement allows students to enter competitive fields. Academic achievement is often a sign of a refined intellect, which can help students in all areas of their lives.

    Righteousness

    It's corny, but true: giving back feels good. Anyone who's participated in fraternity philanthropy knows the feeling of goodwill that comes from giving something back to the community.

    Friendship/Loyalty

    Pi Delta Psi is a fraternity that is unlike any other. We are a brotherhood; a home away from home. We pride ourselves in the strength & authenticity of our bonds.
